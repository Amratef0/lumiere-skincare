Where It Began

A Lab, a Garden, a Dream

Lumière was born in 2018 in a small Parisian apartment — part kitchen laboratory, part rose garden. Our founder, Amélie, spent three years obsessing over one question: why do most skincare products contain ingredients the skin barely recognizes?

The answer became our mission. We set out to create formulas where every ingredient has a purpose — sourced from ethical botanical farms, backed by dermatology, and crafted with the kind of care you'd want for your own skin.

Today, Lumière is trusted by over 12,000 customers in 40+ countries — and every product is still made in small batches to ensure quality you can actually feel.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by three non-negotiables.

🌿
Clean Ingredients
We never use parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrance, or microplastics. Every ingredient is vetted against EWG's Skin Deep database.
🌍
Sustainable Sourcing
We partner with regenerative farms in Morocco, France, and Japan. Our packaging is made from 90% post-consumer recycled materials.
🔬
Clinically Tested
Every formula is dermatologist-tested and validated in independent clinical trials before it ever reaches your hands.
The Good Stuff

Ingredients We Love

We're obsessed with what goes into every drop. Here are the star botanicals you'll find across our range:

Rosehip Seed Oil
Rich in Vitamin C & essential fatty acids. Brightens, firms, and reduces the appearance of fine lines.
Hyaluronic Acid
Draws moisture from the air into skin. Provides up to 72 hours of deep hydration without clogging pores.
Aloe Vera
Our #1 soothing hero. Calms inflammation, reduces redness, and helps skin heal naturally.
Green Tea Extract
A powerhouse antioxidant that fights free radicals, reduces oil production, and protects against UV damage.
Niacinamide
Visibly minimizes pores, fades dark spots, and strengthens the skin barrier over time.
Jojoba Oil
Mimics skin's natural sebum. Balances oil production for both dry and oily skin types.
